ITC 2023

June 6-8, 2023


The fourth Information-Theoretic Cryptography (ITC) conference will take place on June 6-8, 2023 at the Department of Computer Science, Aarhus University, Aarhus, Denmark.

Venue: Department of Computer Science, Aarhus University.


Important Dates

  • Paper submission: Feb 6, 2023
  • Paper acceptance notification: April 17, 2023
  • Conference: June 6-8, 2023
