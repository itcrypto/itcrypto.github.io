In Cooperation With

ITC 2022

July 5-7, 2022



The third Information-Theoretic Cryptography (ITC) conference will take place on July 5-7, 2022. ITC is a conference dedicated to all information-theoretic aspects of cryptography, broadly defined. See the charter for more information.


Conference Format

We are planning for hybrid online/in-person conference. However, the event will be turned into an online-only event should it become clear that in-person participation is not possible and/or unsafe. Moreover, even in case of an in-person event, online attendance will remain possible for speakers and other attendees who do not feel comfortable with in-person attendance.

Important Dates

  • Paper submission: Jan 10, 2022 (11:59pm UTC)
  • Spotlight track nomination deadline: February 28, 2022
  • Paper acceptance notification: April 5, 2022
  • Camera ready version: April 26, 2022
  • Conference: July 5-7, 2022
