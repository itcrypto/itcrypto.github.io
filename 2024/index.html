In Cooperation With

ITC 2024

August 14-16, 2024


The fifth Information-Theoretic Cryptography (ITC) conference will take place on August 14-16, 2024 at Stanford University, Stanford, CA, USA. It is conveniently timed to be just before CRYPTO in Santa Barbara, CA.

Registration

Registration is open now! You can register here.

Pricing:

  • Early Bird (before July 24):
    • General: $250
    • Students: $150
  • Standard (after July 24):
    • General: $300
    • Students: $200

Local Information

  • Location: Stanford's Frances C. Arrillaga Alumni Center. Map here.
  • Accommodation: There are several hotels in downtown Palo Alto. Unfortunately hotels in Palo Alto can be fairly expensive; a more economical option may be shared airbnbs (or shared hotel rooms). In order to facilitate shared housing, we have made a google spreadsheet where participants can share their housing interests and contact information. If you are interested, please contact Mary Wootters at marykw-at-stanford-dot-edu to get access to this spreadsheet.
  • Getting to the venue: The Alumni Center is located on the Stanford campus, about a 20-30-minute walk from downtown Palo Alto. Aside from walking, the Stanford Marguerite Shuttle is a good option; the shuttle is free for everyone (no Stanford ID or anything required).
  • Invitation Letters: If you need an invitation letter, please contact Ruth Harris at rharris3-at-stanford-dot-edu.

We thank for their generous sponsorship.



Code of Conduct

The ITC 2024 Organizers are committed to providing a conference experience free of harassment and discrimination, respecting the dignity of every participant. All participants are expected to abide by the Stanford University code of conduct. If you experience harassment or discriminatory behavior at ITC 2024, we encourage you to reach out to any of the Local Organizers, including Mary Wootters or Dan Boneh. Any action taken by the organizers in this regard, will be with the consent of the complaining party.
