ITC 2020

June 17-19, 2020

Covid19 Update: conference will be online in virtual format


The first Information-Theoretic Cryptography (ITC) conference will take place on June 17-19, 2020. ITC is a new conference dedicated to all information-theoretic aspects of cryptography, broadly defined. See the charter for more information.

Covid19 Update: Due to the ongoing spread of COVID-19 and consequent restrictions on travel, ITC 2020 will move online, to an entirely virtual format. Details will be announced in May. However, we are happy to announce a few specifics:

  • The presentations and discussion sessions will happen online, via Zoom. We will also provide a mechanism for organizing local meetups to watch collectively in locations where gatherings are safe and permitted.
  • Thanks to generous sponsorship from Microsoft, virtual attendance will be entirely free! We will ask attendees to register, and we may limit the number of distinct virtual attendees (with places guaranteed for authors of accepted papers, program committee members, and reviewers).
We look forward to seeing you online in June!
